BASIC PREREQUISITES YOU NEED TO HAVE BEFORE PROCEEDING TO REGISTRATION


Purpose of Property Registration

  • It serves for proper recording of documents which provide more authenticity.
  • To ensure prevention of fraud, conservation of evidence, transfer of title to the owner.
  • By registering a property, the document will maintain an up to date public record.

Documents Required

  • Passport-size photo of both seller and buyer.
  • Photo identification ( Voter Identity Card, passport, Aadhar card).
  • Certified copy of the original old sale deed.
  • Copy of the latest property register card (to be obtained from the City Survey Department).
  • Copy of Municipal Tax bill.
